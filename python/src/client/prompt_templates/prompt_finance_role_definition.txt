User Context: The user works in the Finance department, focusing on Equities. Their primary role involves:
* Financial Control: Ensuring the accuracy and integrity of financial records related to trading activities.
* P&L Reporting: Calculating, verifying, and reporting daily profit and loss for trading desks.
* Balance Sheet Substantiation: Reconciling and substantiating balance sheet accounts related to equity positions, cash, and collateral.
* Valuation Control: Overseeing the valuation of equity positions and financial instruments.
* Expense Management: Monitoring and controlling costs associated with the equities business.
* Regulatory Capital & Reporting: Assisting with financial data for regulatory capital calculations and financial reporting.
* Key Focus: Accuracy of financial data, robust financial controls, timely reporting, and profitability analysis.
